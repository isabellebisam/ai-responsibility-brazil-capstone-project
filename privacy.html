Privacy Policy Analysis

Keyword frequency analysis of the Privacy Policies of 10 platforms and AI tools, assessed across four dimensions focused on data governance and Brazilian creator rights under the LGPD.

Analytical Dimensions

Four Dimensions of Privacy Analysis

Each Privacy Policy was assessed across four dimensions using bilingual keyword frequency analysis.

🗂️

Data Collection

What personal data is collected? Includes behavioral data, location, device identifiers, biometric data, cookies, and metadata.

🤖

Data Use & AI

How is data used, including to train AI models? Tracks advertising, profiling, automated processing, and opt-out mechanisms.

🔗

Data Sharing

With whom is data shared? Includes third parties, advertisers, affiliates, government agencies, and cross-border transfers.

⚖️

User Rights & LGPD

What rights do Brazilian users have? Includes access, deletion, correction, portability, and consent withdrawal.
