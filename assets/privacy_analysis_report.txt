==============================================================================
                             METHODOLOGICAL NOTE                              
==============================================================================

This analysis applies distant reading methodology to Privacy Policy documents.
For platforms governed by multiple documents (Meta: Privacy Policy + Products;
YouTube/Gemini: platform policy + Google Privacy), documents were concatenated
into a single corpus per platform before analysis. This ensures the full scope
of each platform's privacy framework is captured. CapCut's documents were
auto-translated from English to Portuguese via Google Translate prior to
analysis; findings should be interpreted with this limitation in mind.
Bilingual keyword matching (English and Portuguese) is applied in production
mode.
