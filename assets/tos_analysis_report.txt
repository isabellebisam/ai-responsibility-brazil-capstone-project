==============================================================================
                             METHODOLOGICAL NOTE                              
==============================================================================

This analysis employs distant reading methodology (Moretti, 2000) to identify
structural patterns across a corpus of ten legal documents. Keyword frequency
serves as a proxy for the relative emphasis each platform places on a given
dimension of governance. The method is not a substitute for close reading of
specific clauses and should be interpreted in conjunction with the qualitative
analysis in the capstone's main text. Bilingual keyword matching (English and
Portuguese) is used in production mode to handle documents in both languages.
Demo mode uses English translations of representative clauses.

  Reference: Moretti, F. (2000). Conjectures on World Literature. New Left Review, 1, 54–68.
