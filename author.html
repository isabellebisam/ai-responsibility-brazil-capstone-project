About This Project

About This Project

This capstone analyzes how the Terms of Service of 10 major platforms and AI tools affect Brazilian content creators across four dimensions: ownership & licensing, AI training provisions, data governance, and power asymmetry. Using distant reading methodology and bilingual keyword analysis, the research surfaces structural patterns in how platforms govern creator rights — particularly under Brazil's Lei Geral de Proteção de Dados (LGPD) - the Brazilian data protection law.
